Medical Product

Axcel Paracetamol Suspension 250mg

Contents: Paracetamol 250mg in every 5ml

Indications: For the relief of fever, symptoms of cold and influenza, teething pain and headache

Dosage: For oral administration only

Oral suspension: Children 1-6 years old: 2.5-5 ml.

7-12 years old: 5-10 ml. To be taken 3 or 4 times a day.

Children 3 month to 1 year are advised to use Axcel Paracetamol-120 Syrup.
