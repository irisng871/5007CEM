Ashwagandha Extract

Ashwagandha Extract is an adaptogenic herb that supports a healthy stress response and mood.

Directions: As an herbal supplement, adults take one (1) capsule daily with any meal or as directed by a healthcare provider. Do not exceed recommended dosage. Individual results may vary.

Warning: Consult a healthcare provider prior to use if pregnant, nursing, on medications, have a medical condition or are planning a medical procedure. Stop use and contact a physician if adverse reactions occur. Keep out of reach of children. Do not use if product appears to be tampered with or seal is broken. Store at room temperature. No added Sugar, Salt, Yeast, Preservatives or Artificial Flavors. Gluten Free. Non-GMO. Suitable for Vegans.
